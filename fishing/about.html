About This Game

What Makes You Tick: A Stitch in Time is a full-length graphic adventure game by Matthias Kempke and Greg MacWilliam. This fishing mini-game appears as a bonus activity within the full Stitch experience. To play the fishing game in context, play the full (free) demo of What Makes You Tick—A Stitch in Time.

The Tournament

This stand-alone presentation of the Stitch fishing game is a tournament! It's open season in Ravenhollow, and we want to make sure our star fishermen go down in the history books. Therefor, the top-ten high scores of all time will be published in the final player's handbook of What Makes You Tick—A Stitch in Time. Go fish!
